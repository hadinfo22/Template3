fruits chocolate

Wed, May 22th 2024

dried or dried fruits: cranberries, goji berries, blackberries, raisins 120 g. dark chocolate min 70% cocoa 100 g. coconut oil 1 tsp. Break the chocolate into pieces and melt them in a bain-marie with the coconut oil (in a heat-resistant container which you place over a smaller saucepan of very hot water, without the bottom of the container being in contact with water). Spread the melted chocolate evenly on a baking tray covered with baking paper. Sprinkle with a mixture of dried and/or dried fruits. Leave to harden for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.

white chocolate

Wed, May 22th 2024

120 g macadamia butter, 150 g cocoa butter, 150 g erythritol powder** (see substitute above), 20 g powdered milk, 1 tbsp. coffee vanilla powder. Place the macadamia butter and cocoa butter in a glass bowl and place the bowl in a saucepan ⅓ full of water. Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally. When the water boils, reduce the heat slightly and stir until the cocoa butter has completely melted. In a small bowl, mix the powdered sweetener, milk and vanilla powder. Add this mixture to the melted butters, mixing continuously until you obtain a homogeneous and smooth texture. Using a small ladle, distribute this mixture into the chocolate wafer molds and set aside for two hours in the refrigerator. When the wafers have hardened, remove them from the pan and wrap them in parchment paper or aluminum foil. Keep refrigerated.

nuts chocolate

Wed, May 22th 2024

2 eggs, 60 g flour, 100 g butter, 4 g baking powder, 100 g Lindt Dessert chocolate with 70% intense cocoa, 100 g sugar, 1 pinch of salt, 70g roasted hazelnuts. Step 1. Melt the chocolate with the butter in a bain-marie. Step 2. In a mixing bowl, whisk the eggs with the sugar. Add the melted chocolate to the previous mixture then mix. Step 3. Add the flour, yeast and salt then mix until you obtain a homogeneous dough. Add around 50 g of crushed hazelnuts. Step 4. Pour 3/4 of the muffin batter into small paper or silicone cases previously placed in round molds then arrange a few pieces of roasted hazelnuts. Step 5. Bake the muffins at 180°C for around 20 minutes.

truffle chocolate

Wed, May 22th 2024

STEP 1. Break the chocolate into small pieces in a heatproof dish. Melt it in a bain-marie. STEP 2. Gradually add the butter cut into small cubes. Mix. STEP 3. When the butter has melted into the chocolate, remove the dish from the heat, add the egg yolks, vanilla sugar (it adds crunch, if you want a smooth texture, add 10 g of icing sugar and 1 drop of liquid vanilla) and icing sugar. Mix everything well. STEP 4. Put the dough in the fridge for at least an hour so that it solidifies. STEP 5. Then, form small balls of dough by hand, roll them in cocoa then place them on a dish.
